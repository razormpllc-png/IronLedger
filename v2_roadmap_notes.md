# Iron Ledger — v2 Roadmap Notes

## FFL / Dealer Tier
- New pricing tier targeting FFLs and small gun shops (~$14.99/mo or $129.99/yr)
- Bound book (A&D book) style reporting for ATF compliance
- Multi-user access (owner + employees)
- Bulk import/export for inventory management
- Low-stock alerts for ammo and accessories
- Customer transaction logging
- POS integration potential (long-term)

## Desktop Version
- Companion desktop app (macOS + Windows)
- Sync with mobile app via iCloud (macOS) and cloud backend (Windows)
- Larger screen optimized for bulk data entry, inventory management, and reporting
- Better suited for FFL/dealer workflows (bound book entry, printing, etc.)
- Export and printing features are more natural on desktop

## Form 4 Tracker & NFA Community Features
- **Form 4 wait time dashboard:** Visual timeline showing date filed, pending status, and estimated approval window based on community averages
- **Community wait time aggregation:** Anonymized, opt-in data sharing so users can see average approval times by form type, filing method (eForms vs paper), and item category — this is the killer feature the NFA community would flock to
- **ATF status change notifications:** Remind users to check eForms status at configurable intervals, log status changes over time
- **NFA pending items summary view:** Dedicated screen showing all pending items in one place with days waiting, expected approval range, and next steps

## Estate & Trust Features (Pro tier additions)
- **Trust-ready NFA export:** PDF packaging all NFA items, serial numbers, ATF form statuses, responsible persons, and trust name — ready to hand to an attorney
- **Estate planning export:** Full collection summary with purchase prices, estimated current values, serial numbers, provenance, and storage locations — formatted for an attorney or executor
- **Gun trust referral partnership:** Partner with an established gun trust provider (e.g., National Gun Trusts, SilencerShop) for a referral link/discount code inside the app — earns commission without legal liability
- Do NOT build trust creation directly — legal liability risk is too high without attorneys on retainer
